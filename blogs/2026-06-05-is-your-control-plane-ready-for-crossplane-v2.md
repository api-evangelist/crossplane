Crossplane introduces a new `crossplane beta upgrade check` command that scans v1.x control planes to identify any breaking changes before upgrading to v2. While the vast majority of v1.x control planes can upgrade without modifications, the tool surfaces the small subset of features that were discontinued or altered in v2.
